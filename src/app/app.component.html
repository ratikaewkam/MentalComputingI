What is Mental Computing

Mental Computing was born from the idea of ​the boy interested in using AI to help diagnose human mental tendencies. which is a big problem for people nowadays He therefore came up with an idea to help solve the problems and delays of the old system and use technology instead.

Why Mental Computing

Old diagnosis

The system uses a multiple choice questionnaire.

Take a long time.

The system can't recommend nearby hospitals if the patient's condition is worrisome.

Some users were unable to assess their feelings through multiple-choice questionnaires.

Modern diagnosis

Modern systems use AI to process feelings through typing.

Take very short time.
